Vintage Fraisier Box

A classic French fraisier cake, elegantly presented in a luxurious jewelry-box-style biscuit case. You can see the intricate details on this “jewelry box” cake shimmering in the sunlight, thanks to edible pearl luster dust mixed with vodka and hand-painted onto the surface. Inside, it features layers of soft sponge cake, filled with rich custard cream and fresh strawberry jam.

Ingredients

Sponge Cake Base

Cookies & Royal Icing

Strawberries

Others

Follow The Directions

01

Step

Prepare the cookies and royal icing following the Gingerbread House recipe. Once the cookies are baked and cooled, fully cover the surface with white royal icing. Decorate freely as desired. Mix vodka with pearl edible powder. Dip a brush into the mixture and paint the raised details for a shimmering effect. Allow the cookies to dry completely.

02

Step

Assemble four cookie panels into a rectangular box using melted white chocolate as glue. Cut the roll cake into slices that fit snugly inside the cookie box. Place one slice at the bottom of the box. Brush with syrup. Pipe cream over the cake and create a border around the edges. Fill the center with strawberry jam and cover completely with cream.

03

Step

Smooth the surface, then place the next cake layer on top. Brush with syrup and spread an even layer of strawberry jam. Arrange whole fresh strawberries on top. Finish by placing the cookie lid to close the box.
